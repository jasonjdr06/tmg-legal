Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

The Makers Guild Dubai ("TMG", "we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our services.

1. Information We Collect

Our TikTok integration app (TMG Auto Publisher) collects only the minimum information necessary to authenticate and publish content to our own business TikTok account. We do not collect personal information from TikTok viewers, followers, or other users.

2. How We Use Information

Authentication data is used solely to publish pre-approved marketing videos to our official TikTok account on a scheduled basis.

3. Data Storage and Security

Authentication tokens are stored securely on our private servers and are not shared with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any collected information with third parties. Data is only transmitted to TikTok's API for the purpose of content publishing.

5. Data Retention

We retain authentication data only for as long as necessary to operate our content publishing service.

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold.
